I am a Visiting Professor in Mathematics at Davidson College. My current interests are in probabilistic methods, stochastic processes, and machine leanrning applications in data science. I received my PhD in Mathematics from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in August 2023 advised by Mark Walker. My dissertation concerned applications of homological algebra and derived category methods in commutative algebra.

Current Interests

This academic year I am teaching a statistics course with an emphasis on applications in R and data visualization (if you are a student please visit the course Moodle page). Course materials from the statistics course and my coding projects can be found at my GitHub.
